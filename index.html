I teach weekly tutoring sections for one of Berkeley’s CS major prerequisite courses, CS61b. I additionally run an education program for new developers in Blueprint, a Berkeley CS club for social good.

When I step away from my computer, I'm usually out rock climbing in Yosemite or cycling the Bay Area. I’m presently on the lookout for SWE internships and computing research opportunities. My email and resume are above, please don't hesitate to reach out!
